About Me

Qusai Ismail is pursuing his M.Sc. in computer science at the Jordan University of Science and Technology. received his B.Sc. Degree in Computer Science from Yarmouk University in 2019 with honors. His research interests include Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, deep learning, computer Vision, Pattern Recognition, model interpretation, and Natural Language Processing. He is also a researcher in the field of natural language processing (NLP) and computational linguistics, and he is known for his work in lexical complexity prediction. He co-authored a paper for the SemEval-2021 competition, specifically working on Task 1, which involved predicting lexical complexity. This task is essential for applications like text simplification and reformatting, where understanding the complexity level of words and phrases can enhance accessibility and comprehension in various contexts. The team, including Qusai, utilized advanced NLP models like BERT and RoBERTa, achieving impressive accuracy and ranking highly in the competition with their innovative approach
